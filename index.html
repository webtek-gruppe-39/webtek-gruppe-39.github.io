Our client, Bodø Fiskeklubb (imaginary), is a local fishing-team that wants a website to attract new members and information about activities for existing members

Information about the club

Bodø fishing clubs, an organization for hobby fishermen in Bodø. The organization's purpose is to increase interest in fishing. The club organizes regular trips, courses and other social events that all club members can take part in upon registration. In addition, it is also possible to rent fishing equipment for all club members. The club consists of older experienced anglers as well as younger beginners. Membership fee costs NOK 600 annually.

Purpose and goals

Spread information about fishing and increase the interest for fishing within Bodø

Audience

Old and new potensial members of Bodø Fiskeklubb

Content and how it is organized

  • Overview of different types of fishing (and where they can be fished): Cod, Pollock, Mackerel, Salmon, Trout
  • Fishing equipment that can be bought/rented on the sitr: Fishing rods, swallows, nylon, and other small things, Whoa, Knife, Small boat, Lifejacket, Yacht
  • Blogs/info on how to prepare fish: Fish soup, Fried trout on a pan
  • Fishing license: Overview of areas where a fishing license is required, Fishing licenses can also be purchased from the club/site
  • Leaderboard (Club Record): Overview of previous records
  • pictures from SOME: - Use # (something) and get your photo uploaded to the site's archive
  • map of fishing spots
  • fetch info from the web about the weather, high tide etc.
  • become a member: form to sign up with feedback
  • Contact the club

Functional and non-functional requirements

  • Responsive design for desktop only. Mobile no concern.
